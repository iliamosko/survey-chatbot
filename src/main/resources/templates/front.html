Directions: For the collaborative portion of this study, you will participate in a visual judgement ability task with your partner. One partner will be the “feedback-giver” and the other will be the “feedback-taker”. For this round, you have been assigned the role of “feedback-taker” and your partner has been assigned to the role of “feedback-giver”. The “feedback-giver” will have the opportunity to provide the “feedback-taker” with feedback at several points during the task, which you can then use to amend your responses. However, only your final answer will count towards your team’s score.
